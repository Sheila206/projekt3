About me

I’m currently studying Multimedia Design at UCL, where I’m developing my skills in digital design, photography and visual storytelling. I’m drawn to warm, simple aesthetics and the small details that give a design its personality. I love adding small personal touches to my work — details that make a visual feel warm, intentional and uniquely mine. I aim to create designs that feel atmospheric, engaging and thoughtfully crafted. I enjoy collaborating with others, learning new tools and bringing ideas to life with a structured and thoughtful approach. I’m excited to keep growing my skills and contribute to creative projects where care, quality and visual identity truly matter.
